Our background

Testicon is dedicated to helping people excel and succeed through certification. Our globally recognized credentials are aimed at enhancing individual productivity, marketability, and value.
Get certified and get noticed.

Who we are

Testicon is a leader in providing performance-based certification exams for IT Professionals and students who want to start a carrer in IT. We have 2 Testicon Authorized Testing Centers in Sweden and Denmark.

Established in 2005, Testicon provides career-oriented certification exams to IT Professionals and aspirants who want to start a carrer. We provide you with an oppurtunity to take industry desired certification tests in Java certification, Angular certification and the SQL Server Application Development certification. Testicon currently delivers more than 8500 certification exams each year.

What we do

Testicon works with industry-leading certification providers to bring their programs successfully to market. With a particular expertise in academia, Testicon provides IT Professionals with exams in certification in programing. In the highly-competitive IT world, it is important that a programmer can verify his/her skills to future and current employers. We provide them with a platform to get highly-desired certifications for a brilliant career in IT.

How we do it

Testicon delivers certification exams through a specialized network of Testicon Authorized Testing Centers in Sweden.

Centers are uniquely positioned to provide the highest level of reliable, performance-based testing.
